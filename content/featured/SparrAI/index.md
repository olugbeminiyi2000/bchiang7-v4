Sparr AI is a voice-first mock interview platform that creates a realistic, dynamic interview experiences. Instead of scripted questions, it uses **Vertex AI** to analyze your resume and identify weaknesses, while **ElevenLabs** powers distinct interviewer personas that challenge you in real-time across 10+ languages. Features include session spaces that remember your past performance and detailed timestamped feedback reports.
